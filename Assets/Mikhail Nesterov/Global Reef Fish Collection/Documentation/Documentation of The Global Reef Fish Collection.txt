Global Reef Fish Collection Pack


The Pack brings a vibrant collection of 24 marine fish from around the world to your Unity project. Perfect for game developers, AR/VR creators, and environment designers, this pack adds authentic diversity and color to any underwater scene. All models are optimized for real-time use and ideal for mobile, desktop, or console applications.


Each fish has an animation loop. 
There are 3 kinds of textures: Albedo, Metallic, Normal. 2K resolution
